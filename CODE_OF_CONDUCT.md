# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Everyone participating in the Procwright project — in issues, pull requests, discussions, and review — is expected to
make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints; disagree on technical grounds, not personal ones.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or review.

## Enforcement

Report violations privately to the maintainer: use the
[private vulnerability reporting form](https://github.com/Ulviar/Procwright/security/advisories/new) (it reaches the
maintainer directly) or contact [@Ulviar](https://github.com/Ulviar) on GitHub. Reports are reviewed confidentially.
The maintainer may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that violate this code, and may temporarily or
permanently ban participants for repeated or severe violations.
